About ConnectHub

ConnectHub is a community business network platform designed to help local businesses and professionals connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Our mission is to foster meaningful relationships within your community by providing a platform where businesses and professionals can showcase their work, discover opportunities, and build lasting connections.

Features

  • Business listings with detailed profiles
  • Professional profiles showcasing skills and expertise
  • Advanced search and discovery tools
  • Secure OAuth authentication
  • Privacy-first design
